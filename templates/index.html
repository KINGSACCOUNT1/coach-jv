Everything you need to know about CoachJVTech

Our team of professional traders executes strategic trades on your behalf using proven strategies. You invest in a pool, and profits from successful trades are distributed proportionally to all investors in that pool.

We charge a 2% annual management fee and a 20% performance fee on profits only. If you don't make money, we don't make money beyond the small management fee.

Each pool has a minimum lock period (30-90 days depending on the pool). After the lock period expires, you can withdraw your funds plus profits at any time with 24-48 hour processing.

We use industry-leading security measures including cold storage, multi-signature wallets, and 2FA authentication. All trades are transparent and auditable on the blockchain. However, crypto trading carries inherent risks.

Minimum investments vary by pool: $500 for Conservative, $1,000 for Balanced, and $2,500 for Aggressive pools. There is no maximum limit.
